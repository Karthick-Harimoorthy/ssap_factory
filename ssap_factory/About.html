About SSAP

Sai Saran Automotive Products (SSAP) is a precision CNC auto components company based in Hosur, Tamil Nadu.

We specialize in the production of spur gears, pinion gears, precision shafts, cylindrical pins, bushings, and custom-turned components for the automotive and industrial sectors. Our shop floor operates a fleet of CNC turning centres and vertical machining centres (VMCs), all running advanced FANUC control systems.

Our quality system is anchored by a Master Samples Board — every product variant we deliver has an approved reference part displayed on the production floor. Operators match every batch to the master, ensuring dimensional consistency across thousands of parts.

Located at No. 8/A, New Sidco Industries Estate, Sri Nagar, Hosur – 635 109, we are well-positioned to serve OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers across Tamil Nadu and Karnataka.
